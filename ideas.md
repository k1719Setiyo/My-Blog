<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Swiss Style (International Typographic Style)
  **Core Principles**: Objectivity, readability, asymmetry, mathematical grids.
  **Color Philosophy**: Stark white background, black text, and a single bold primary color (e.g., Electric Blue #0055FF) for interactions to denote function without emotion.
  **Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetrical layouts with strong vertical axes. Content is aligned to a strict grid but often leaves columns empty for breathing room.
  **Signature Elements**: Large, flush-left typography; thick horizontal rules to separate sections; absence of decorative icons.
  **Interaction Philosophy**: Instant, sharp transitions. Hover states are binary (on/off) with no fade-in, emphasizing precision.
  **Animation**: None, or extremely fast (0.1s) linear translations.
  **Typography System**: *Helvetica Now* (or *Inter*) for everything. Huge, bold headings (80px+) contrasted with small, dense body copy (14px) with tight tracking.
</idea>
